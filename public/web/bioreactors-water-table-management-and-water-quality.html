Bioreactors, Water Table Management, and Water Quality

Skills: HTML, CSS, ColdFusion, Photoshop, jQuery

View Site Â»

The Bioreactors, Water Table Management, and Water Quality is a simple site with the goal of educating people about controlled drainage and bioreactors and how they can be used to limit the amount of nutrient loading into surface water, and to manage soil moisture during drought conditions.

I was responsible for creating the design and fully coding this site. The site is fully responsive and ties into an in-house CMS. Because the content can be rather dry (pun intended) I tried to make the site engaging through the use of large imagery, interactive charts, and simple typography. I was responsible for the Spanish version of the site as well.
